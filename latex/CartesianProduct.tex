\section{DEFINIZIONE}
Given two sets $A$ and $B$, the Cartesian product $A \times B$ is the set of all ordered pairs $(a,b)$ where $a \in A$ and 
$b \in B$.

\section{NOTAZIONE}
$A \times B = {(a,b) | a \in A and b \in B }$

1 Neighborhood of a point

1.1 Definition

Let X be a metric space. All points and sets mentioned below are understood to be elements and subsets of X.

A neighborhood of p is a set Nr(p) consisting of all q such that d(p,q) < r, for some r > 0. The number r is called the radius of Nr(p).
